SymbolicModularGamma0EtaQuotient holds data to compute an eta quotient expansions of $F_{s, r, m, t}(gamma tau)$ at all cusps of $Gamma_0(N)$. See eqref{eq:F_s-r-m-t(gamma*tau)}.

etaQuotient: (PositiveInteger, List Integer, PositiveInteger, List Integer, PositiveInteger, NonNegativeInteger) -> %

etaQuotient(nn, s, mm, r, m, t) represents the expansion of $F_{r,s,m,t}(gamma tau)$ for all gamma corresponding to the cusps of $Gamma_0(nn)$. $r$ and $s$ are indexed by the divisors of $mm$ and $nn$, repectively.
