QEtaIdealHemmecke(C, L)¶

qetaih.spad line 95

  • C: EuclideanDomain
  • L: Join(UnivariateLaurentSeriesCategory C, QEtaGradedAlgebra C)

QEtaIdealHemmecke implements functions to computed an ideal of relations among Dedekind eta functions of level N. The computation of an algebra basis of eta quotient of level N is done in an extended way so that there is no need to compute a Gröbner basis of the relations among the eta quotions in order to represent them in terms of the input eta quotients.
