SymbolicEtaQuotientLambdaGamma¶

qetaquotsymb.spad line 327 [edit on github]

SymbolicEtaQuotientLambdaGamma holds data to compute an eta quotient expansion of g_{r,m,lambda}(gamma tau). See eqref{eq:g_r-m-lambda(gamma*tau)} and eqref{eq:g_r-m—lambda(gamma*tau)}.

etaQuotient: (PositiveInteger, List Integer, PositiveInteger, NonNegativeInteger, Matrix Integer) -> %

etaQuotient(mm, r, m, lambda, gamma) represents the expansion of $g_{r,m,lambda}(gamma tau)$ in terms of $x = exp(2 pi i tau/w)$ where w=width(nn, c) and gamma=matrix[[a,b],[c,d]] represents the cusp a/c. $r$ is indexed by the divisors of $mm$.

etaQuotient: (QEtaSpecification, PositiveInteger, NonNegativeInteger, Matrix Integer) -> %

etaQuotient(rspec, m, lambda, gamma) represents the expansion of $g_{r,m,lambda}(gamma tau)$ in terms of $x = exp(2 pi i tau/w)$ where w=width(nn, c) and gamma=matrix[[a,b],[c,d]] represents the cusp a/c. $r$ is given by rspec.

qExponent: % -> Fraction Integer

qExponent(e) returns the order of the expansion of e in q = exp(2 pi i tau) while neglecting the (ctau+d) factor. It corresponds to the exponent of the fourth product of eqref{eq:g_r-m-lambda(gamma*tau)}. Compare also with eqref{eq:modular-g_r(gamma*tau)}.

rationalPrefactor: % -> Fraction Integer

rationalPrefactor(x) returns the square of the second product in eqref{eq:g_r-m-lambda(gamma*tau)}.

unityExponent: % -> Fraction Integer

unityExponent(e) returns $sum_{delta} rdelta*(vdelta+Kappa_delta)/24$. It corresponds to the second factor of eqref{eq:g_r-m-lambda(gamma*tau)}.
